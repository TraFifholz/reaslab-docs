# Reaslab Introduction

ReasLab enables you to work with complex mathematical and optimization problems through natural language interaction, automated model construction, and seamless integration of computation and documentation.

## Key Features

- **Natural Language Input**: Describe complex mathematical or optimization problems directly in natural language.

- **Automated Model Construction**: Let ReasLab construct executable models and run appropriate solvers automatically.

- **Structured Content Generation**: Generate structured technical content alongside computations without manual formatting.

- **Continuous Workspace**: Iterate on reasoning, documentation, and results within a single continuous workspace.

- **Seamless Workflow**: Move seamlessly from exploration to verified conclusions and publication-ready outputs.
